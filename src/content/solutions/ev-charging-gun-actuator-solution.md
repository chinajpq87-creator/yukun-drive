## The Challenge

EV charging gun manufacturers face stringent requirements for the electronic locking mechanism. The actuator must reliably lock the connector during charging, prevent accidental disconnection, and comply with international safety standards — all in a compact, weather-exposed package.

| Challenge | Why It Matters | Our Approach |
|:---|:---|:---|
| **Self-Locking Reliability** | Safety standards (IEC 62196, GB/T 20234) require the connector to remain locked even if power is lost | Worm gear self-locking — mechanical hold, zero power required |
| **Environmental Exposure** | Charging guns operate outdoors: rain, dust, -30°C to +85°C | IP67-rated micro switches, sealed gearboxes, validated -30°C to +85°C |
| **High Cycle Life** | Public charging stations see 10-50 insertions/day → 100K+ cycles/year | Extended life worm gear design, 200K+ cycle validated |
| **Compact Integration** | Charging gun handles have severe space constraints | N20-based actuators: 12mm motor + slim worm gearbox, custom housing |
| **Automotive Compliance** | Tier-1 suppliers require IATF 16949 quality systems | IATF 16949 certified production lines available |
| **Supply Chain Simplicity** | Managing motor + switch + housing from 3 suppliers adds cost and risk | Pre-assembled motor + micro switch + housing assemblies |

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Can you provide a complete actuator assembly, not just the motor?**
A: Yes. Our ASM-01 assembly includes N20 worm motor + IP67 micro switch + housing + automotive connector, 100% tested, at $12.00 FOB (1K+). This is our recommended solution — one supplier, one quality system.

**Q: Does the worm gear really stay locked without power?**
A: Yes. The worm gear geometry provides inherent self-locking — the output gear cannot back-drive the worm. This is a mechanical property, not dependent on electronics, making it ideal for safety-critical charging applications.

**Q: What automotive documentation do you provide?**
A: For IATF 16949 programs, we provide PPAP Level 3 documentation, including: dimensional reports, material certifications, process FMEA, control plan, capability studies (Cpk), and IMDS data.

**Q: Can you customize the locking travel distance?**
A: Yes. We customize the output shaft, cam profile, and limit switch position to match your required locking travel. Standard configurations support 5-15mm linear travel.

**Q: What about EMI/EMC compliance?**
A: DC brushed motors generate electrical noise. We offer ferrite-suppressed configurations for sensitive applications. For ultra-low EMI, we recommend our BLDC-based actuator options.

**Q: What's the lead time for production orders?**
A: Standard configurations: 2-3 weeks for 1K units. Custom assemblies: 4-6 weeks for first production run. We maintain safety stock for repeat customers.

**Q: Do you support the Chinese GB/T market?**
A: Yes. Our factory is in China and our actuators are fully compliant with GB/T 20234.1/.2/.3 standards. We supply motors to several Chinese EV charger manufacturers.
