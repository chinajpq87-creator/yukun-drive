## Selection Guide

### Step 1: Define Your Requirements

```
What are you driving?       →  □ Rotary motion  □ Linear motion  □ Fluid handling
What torque/force?          →  Continuous: ___ N·m / Peak: ___ N·m
What speed range?           →  ___ to ___ RPM
Space constraints?          →  Max Ø: ___ mm / Max L: ___ mm
Noise budget?               →  □ <30dB (silent)  □ <40dB (quiet)  □ Not critical
Duty cycle?                 →  □ Continuous  □ Intermittent  □ Occasional
Regulatory requirements?    →  □ ISO 13485  □ FDA  □ CE  □ None specific
Sterilization method?       →  □ EtO  □ Gamma  □ Autoclave  □ None
Estimated annual volume?    →  □ Prototype (<100)  □ Pilot (100-1K)  □ Production (>1K)
```
