# Micro Drive Solutions for Robotics Joints

**Precision gear motors for robot grippers, educational robots, and servo replacements.**

## The Challenge

Robotics applications demand precision, repeatability, and torque density in the smallest possible package. Off-the-shelf hobby servos often lack the precision and reliability needed for serious applications.

| Challenge | Why It Matters | Our Approach |
|:---|:---|:---|
| **Precision** | Joint positioning requires low backlash and repeatability | Planetary gears with <1° backlash |
| **Torque Density** | Small robots need high torque in minimal space | N20 platform: 3.0 kg·cm in 12mm package |
| **Position Feedback** | Closed-loop control is essential | Hall encoders (7/12 PPR) and magnetic encoders (up to 512 CPR) |
