## The Challenge

Smart lock manufacturers face demanding electromechanical requirements. The motor is the critical actuator that determines lock reliability, battery life, and user experience.

| Challenge | Why It Matters | Our Approach |
|:---|:---|:---|
| **Battery Life** | Smart locks must operate 6-12 months on 4×AA batteries | Low-current motor designs drawing <200mA under load |
| **Stall Protection** | Locked-rotor conditions during bolt jam must not burn the motor | Over-current protection + mechanical self-locking options |
| **Noise Control** | Users expect near-silent operation in residential settings | Precision-ground metal gears, <50dB @30cm |
| **Size Constraints** | European/US slim-profile locks demand motors under 12mm thick | N20 series from 12mm diameter, custom slim gearboxes |
| **Cycle Life** | Hotel locks require 100,000+ cycles without failure | Extended life testing, 200K+ cycle rated configurations |
| **Supply Consistency** | Multi-year product lifecycles demand consistent motor supply | Single-source N20 platform, 7 sub-series, 20-year factory |

## Selection Guide

### Step 1: Define Your Lock Type

```
Lock type?                  →  □ Residential fingerprint  □ Hotel card  □ Deadbolt  □ Glass door  □ Safe
Drive mechanism?            →  □ Rotary bolt  □ Linear bolt  □ Latch retraction
Power source?               →  □ 4×AA (6V)  □ 2×CR123A (6V)  □ Li-ion (3.7V)  □ 12V wired
Battery life target?        →  □ 6 months  □ 12 months  □ 24 months
Anti-tamper requirement?    →  □ Yes (need self-locking)  □ No
Estimated annual volume?    →  □ Prototype (<100)  □ Pilot (1K-5K)  □ Production (>10K)
```

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: What's the typical motor cost for a smart lock BOM?**
A: For residential fingerprint locks, the motor typically represents $2-5 of the BOM. Our N20 metal spur motors start at $2.80 FOB at volume, with configurations scaling based on precision and features.

**Q: Can you match the motor dimensions of our current supplier?**
A: Yes. Send us your current motor drawing or sample, and we'll match the key dimensions — shaft diameter, length, mounting hole pattern, and overall envelope.

**Q: What's the minimum order quantity for custom configurations?**
A: We support prototype quantities (3-5 units) for evaluation. Custom shaft/connector MOQ is 1,000 units. Standard configurations ship from 200 units.

**Q: How do you ensure consistent quality across production batches?**
A: Every motor undergoes 100% load testing with automated parameter recording. We provide batch-level performance data and material traceability certificates.

**Q: What about battery life optimization?**
A: We work with your lock electronics team to optimize the motor winding for your specific voltage and duty cycle. Low-current windings (<80mA no-load) are available for battery-powered locks.

**Q: Do you provide the motor with integrated position sensing?**
A: Yes. Hall effect encoders (7 PPR or 12 PPR) are available for closed-loop position control. Magnetic encoders with higher CPR are available for premium applications.

**Q: What certifications do you have for the European/US smart lock market?**
A: Our motors are RoHS and REACH compliant. IATF 16949 certified production lines are available for automotive-grade quality requirements. CE/UL certification support for the complete lock assembly is available through our engineering partners.
