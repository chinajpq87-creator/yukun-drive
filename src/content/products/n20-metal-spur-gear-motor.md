## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: What's the difference between N20 metal spur and N20 plastic spur?**
A: Metal spur gears offer higher torque capacity (0.8-2.0 vs 0.3-1.0 kg·cm), longer life, and better temperature resistance. Plastic spur is lighter and quieter but for lower-load applications.

**Q: Can I get the motor in 3V for a single-cell Li-ion battery?**
A: Yes. 3V winding is available. The no-load speed is ~7,000 RPM before gear reduction. Specify your battery voltage when requesting a sample.

**Q: What's the lifespan of this motor?**
A: Typical service life is 50,000–100,000 cycles under rated load, depending on duty cycle and operating conditions. For applications requiring 200K+ cycles, we recommend the N20 Planetary variant.

**Q: Can you provide a matched pair (left-hand/right-hand rotation)?**
A: Yes. Simply reverse the polarity — the motor runs in either direction. For simultaneous dual-motor applications, we can provide matched-speed pairs.

**Q: What's the difference vs. the N20 Planetary?**
A: Spur gear = simpler, more cost-effective, higher backlash. Planetary = precision, lower backlash, higher torque density, higher cost. Choose spur for cost-sensitive high-volume applications; choose planetary for precision applications.

**Q: Do you support drop-shipping to our contract manufacturer?**
A: Yes. We ship globally to your designated assembly location. Standard export packaging with moisture protection.
