# Yukun Drive YouTube Channel Metadata Design

## Goal

Create review-ready YouTube channel metadata that attracts product engineers through teardown, BOM, and micro-motion engineering content.

## Approved channel positioning

- Channel name: `Yukun Drive`
- Handle: `@YukunDrive`
- Audience: product engineers and sourcing teams.
- Content focus: consumer-product teardowns, component BOM analysis, and micro-motion engineering insights.
- Service role: engineering-led product selection and supply coordination.

## Public messaging boundaries

- Do not name Yibao or any manufacturing partner.
- Do not claim that Yukun is a manufacturer.
- Do not promise fixed samples, response time, lead time, Incoterms, capacity, or certifications.
- State that product availability, lead time, and commercial terms are confirmed for each inquiry.

## Deliverable

Create a review-only English metadata document with:

1. Banner headline: `Micro Motion Teardowns · BOMs · Engineering Insights`
2. Banner subheadline: `For Product Engineers & Sourcing Teams`
3. Search-optimized channel description with website and contact links.
4. Channel keywords targeting micro-motor teardown, BOM, gear-motor selection, smart-lock motors, and robotics actuators.
5. A YouTube Studio paste checklist.

## Verification

Review the copy for the prohibited claims and partner names. Do not create a channel, publish content, or modify an external account.
