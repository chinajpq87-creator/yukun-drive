# Yukun Homepage Positioning Design

## Goal

Position Yukun as the customer-facing engineering and supply service provider for micro-motion components. Products are supplied through established manufacturing partners, whose identities are not shown on the public website.

## Approved public messaging

- Yukun provides engineering-led product selection, inquiry coordination, quotation support, sample coordination, and order follow-up.
- Product availability, specifications, lead time, and commercial terms are confirmed for each inquiry.
- The homepage may describe component categories and application value, but must not name a manufacturing partner.

## Content boundaries

- Do not imply that Yukun owns or operates a factory.
- Do not disclose the name, contact details, capacity, or location details of a manufacturing partner.
- Remove fixed commitments for sample turnaround, response time, production lead time, Incoterms, certifications, manufacturing history, plant size, and daily capacity unless separately verified and explicitly approved.
- Replace manufacturing claims with neutral service wording such as “manufacturing partner network” and “engineering-led sourcing support”.

## Homepage changes

1. Replace the hero credential strip with a neutral engineering-and-sourcing message.
2. Rewrite the “Why Yukun” section around service coordination, product matching, custom requirement communication, quality-document coordination, logistics coordination, and engineer-led support.
3. Rewrite the consultation CTA to state that product, delivery, and commercial details are confirmed per inquiry.
4. Preserve navigation, products, applications, solutions, blog content, and page structure.
