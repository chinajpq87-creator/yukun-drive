# LinkedIn Profile Positioning Design

## Goal

Position the profile owner as an experienced sales manager in China’s micro-motor component industry and as the founder of the independent Yukun Drive trade service.

## Approved identity

- Current role: Sales Manager in the sales department of a leading Chinese manufacturer of core micro-motor components.
- Independent role: Founder of Yukun Drive.
- Public employer treatment: describe the employer generically; do not publish the Yibao company name.
- Professional scope: micro switches, commutators, carbon-brush assemblies, terminals, micro motors, and related motion components.

## Approved headline

```text
Sales Manager at a Leading Chinese Micro-Motor Component Manufacturer | Founder of Yukun Drive | Product Matching & Supply Coordination
```

## About structure

1. Establish manufacturing-side sales experience and practical understanding of product and customer requirements.
2. List the relevant component categories without revealing the employer.
3. Explain that Yukun Drive is an independently operated trade service for product matching, supply-resource coordination, quotation support, sample follow-up, and order coordination.
4. Close with the website, contact email, and a statement that specifications, availability, lead time, and commercial terms are confirmed for each inquiry.

## Public messaging boundaries

- Do not name Yibao or imply that Yukun Drive is its official overseas channel.
- Do not claim that Yukun Drive owns manufacturing facilities.
- Do not present the profile owner as an engineer.
- Do not disclose internal prices, customer names, drawings, capacity, or non-public employer information.
- Do not promise fixed response, sample, production, or delivery timing.

## Verification

Create review-only LinkedIn copy and scan it for prohibited names and fixed commitments. Do not open, edit, save, or publish a LinkedIn profile.
